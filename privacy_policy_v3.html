Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Med Attendance ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

By using Med Attendance, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Age Requirements

Med Attendance is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are under 16, please do not use this App or provide any personal information. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will delete that information promptly.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use Med Attendance, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Full name, mobile phone number (with country code), and password
  • Profile Information:(Optional) Profile photo, graduation year, field of study, educational status, and location/region
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and verification codes (OTP)

Attendance Data

  • Attendance records (presence/absence logs)

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information to provide and improve our services:

  • Device UUID: A randomly generated unique identifier created and stored locally on your device upon first use. This UUID is also registered on our servers to securely link your account to a specific device. This helps prevent unauthorized account access and ensures that students cannot log in or attend sessions on behalf of others. If you clear your app data, reinstall the app, or change devices, you may need to verify your identity and update your registered device before logging in again. This can be done through our support team or designated in-app verification process. By creating an account, you explicitly consent to linking your account to a single device for academic integrity, security, and anti-impersonation purposes.
  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system and version
  • App version and build number

Bluetooth and NFC Data

To enable attendance tracking functionality, the App broadcasts limited identification data for academic verification purposes:

  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE): A unique identifier (user ID) and your display name are temporarily transmitted to nearby attendance readers when you enable Bluetooth attendance mode.
  • NFC Card Emulation: Your unique identifier (user ID) is transmitted when you tap your device on an NFC attendance reader.

Important: This data is only broadcast while you actively use the attendance feature, and is limited to your user ID and display name solely for verifying your presence in sessions. It is not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Attendance Tracking: To record and manage your attendance at educational sessions
  • Profile Management: To maintain and display your profile information
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

Permissions We Request

Permission Purpose
Internet Required to communicate with our servers and sync your data
Bluetooth Required to broadcast your digital ID to attendance readers for proximity-based attendance
NFC Required to emulate a digital ID card for tap-based attendance
Photo Library Optional - used only if you choose to select an existing photo for your profile

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Sensitive data (authentication tokens, device identifiers) is stored using encrypted secure storage on your device
  • Non-sensitive preferences (display name, profile settings) are stored locally for offline access

Data Sharing

Educational Institutions

We share your attendance data with the educational institutions where you are enrolled. This includes:

  • Your name and identification
  • Attendance records for your enrolled classes

This sharing is necessary to fulfill the core purpose of the App - enabling educational institutions to track and manage student attendance.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all your personal data is immediately and permanently deleted from our servers

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update

You can view and update your profile information at any time through the App settings.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time by:

  • Using the account deletion feature in the App
  • Visiting: https://sm.med-attendance.online/learners/delete-account

Upon deletion, all your personal data will be immediately removed from our systems.

Opt-Out of Attendance Broadcasting

You can stop Bluetooth and NFC broadcasting at any time by:

  • Turning off Bluetooth or NFC in your device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
