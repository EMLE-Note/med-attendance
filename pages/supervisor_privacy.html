1. Introduction

Welcome to Attendance Supervisor ("App," "Service," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use the Attendance Supervisor mobile application. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our Service.

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register for an account and use the App, you provide us with the following personal information:

Information Purpose
Full Name Account identification and display
Mobile Phone Number Account registration, verification, and communication
Password Account security (stored in encrypted/hashed form)
Job Title Professional profile information
Professional Field Professional profile information
Profile Photo Account personalization (optional)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

Information Purpose
Device Identifier (UUID) Device recognition and session management
Device Type Service optimization and support
Operating System & Version Compatibility and troubleshooting
App Version Version management and updates

2.3 Attendance Data

When you use the App for attendance tracking, the following data is collected:

  • Student/Trainee ID and Name
  • Check-in/Check-out Timestamps
  • NFC Card Identifiers
  • Bluetooth Device Identifiers
  • Attendance Status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create and manage your account
  • Service Provision: Provide attendance tracking functionality
  • Authentication: Verify your identity and secure your account
  • Communication: Send service-related notifications
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud and security incidents

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Your Institution

We share attendance data and your professional profile with the institution(s) you are affiliated with. This is necessary for the core functionality of the App.

4.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Communication platforms (Telegram for verification)

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

5.2 Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers. By using the App, you consent to the transfer and storage of your data on these servers.

6. Data Ownership

  • Your Personal Data: You retain ownership of your personal profile information
  • Attendance Data: Owned by the institution you are affiliated with
  • Application Data: The App provider owns aggregated, anonymized usage data

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Attendance Records: May be retained by institutions as required

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View your personal data through the App
  • Update: Correct or update your profile information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

Account Deletion

You can delete your account through:

  • The account deletion feature in the App settings
  • Contacting our support team

9. Children's Privacy

The Attendance Supervisor App is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Telegram: For account verification and notifications
  • Google Firebase: For authentication and cloud services

Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

12. Offline Data and Synchronization

The App stores attendance data locally when offline. This data is automatically synchronized when connectivity is restored. We recommend using device-level security to protect locally stored data.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • The updated policy will be posted in the App
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • For significant changes, we may provide additional notice

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
