Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kader ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Age Requirements

Important

Kader is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are under 16, please do not use this App or provide any personal information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use Kader, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Full name, mobile phone number (with country code), and password
  • Profile Information (Optional): Profile photo, graduation year, field of study, educational status, and location/region
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and verification codes (OTP)

Attendance Data

  • Attendance records (presence/absence logs)

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information to provide and improve our services:

  • Device UUID: A randomly generated unique identifier created and stored locally on your device upon first use. This UUID is registered on our servers to link your account to your device and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system and version
  • App version and build number

Bluetooth and NFC Data

To enable attendance tracking functionality, the App broadcasts limited identification data:

  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE): Your user ID and name are broadcast to nearby attendance readers when you enable Bluetooth attendance mode
  • NFC Card Emulation: Your user ID is transmitted when you tap your device on an NFC attendance reader

This data is broadcast only when you actively use the attendance feature and is limited to your user ID and name for identification purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Attendance Tracking: To record and manage your attendance at educational sessions
  • Profile Management: To maintain and display your profile information
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

Permissions We Request

Permission Purpose
Internet Required to communicate with our servers and sync your data
Bluetooth Required to broadcast your digital ID to attendance readers
NFC Required to emulate a digital ID card for tap-based attendance
Photo Library Optional - used only if you choose to select a profile photo

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Sensitive data (authentication tokens, device identifiers) is stored using encrypted secure storage on your device
  • Non-sensitive preferences (display name, profile settings) are stored locally for offline access

Data Sharing

Educational Institutions

We share your attendance data with the educational institutions where you are enrolled. This includes:

  • Your name and identification
  • Attendance records for your enrolled classes

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all your personal data is immediately and permanently deleted from our servers

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update

You can view and update your profile information at any time through the App settings.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time by:

  • Using the account deletion feature in the App
  • Visiting: https://sm.med-attendance.online/learners/delete-account

Opt-Out of Attendance Broadcasting

You can stop Bluetooth and NFC broadcasting at any time by turning off Bluetooth or NFC in your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
